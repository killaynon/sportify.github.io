Football

Arguably the most famous sport in the world, Football. Also known as Soccer, dates back to more than 2000 years ago to ancient China.

Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. This sport can be played anywhere as long as one has a round ball and posts

There are over a 100 leagues across the world. Yes, Football is a global sport. Nothing brings the world together like Football especially the world cup which holds the record as the most viewed sporting event across the world

Football loves talents and have their own individual honours given to the best players for the year like Ballon D'or, FIFA the Best and UEFA player of the year award.

Some of the most prestigious clubs in the world are Manchester United, Barcelona, Real Madrid, Bayern Munich.
