Basketball

The history of basketball began with its invention in 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts by Canadian physical instructor, James Naismith as a less injury-prom sport than football.

The American National Basketball Association (NBA), established in 1946, grew to a multibillion-dollar enterprise by the end of the century.

Basketball is a focal sport in America with so many little children, both boys and girls, playing on the street courts of America. Not only children but University students find this sport as good leisure activity.

A few basketball franchises in America are The Los Angeles Lakers, Brooklyn Nets, San Anonio Spurs and so on. This sport isn't national but on a global stage with competitions like the FIBA world cup for all nations and the Euroleague for European teams.
